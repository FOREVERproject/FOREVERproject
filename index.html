The Forever Era

The crystals were ancient, buried deep in the earth, waiting for the ones who could unlock their secrets. After which it took decades of research and experimentation that lead to a critical breakthrough where they gave off an essence with temporal properties.

Eventually scientists worked to harness the essence which put them into spinning levitations, and allowed them to develop a new type of processing unit called TIPS (Temporal Intelligence Processing Shapes).

These TIPS, which people also referred to as Forever Shapes due to their seamless and fluid rotations, sparked a technological revolution, leading to advancements in fields such as energy, computing, artificial intelligence, gene editing and faster-than-light travel.

This new era, known as the "Forever", marked a significant turning point in human history, altering the course of humanity's destiny and purpose.
